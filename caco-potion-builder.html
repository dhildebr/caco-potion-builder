CACO Potion Builder

Kryptopyr's Complete Alchemy and Cooking Overhaul makes rarer ingredients confer more potent alchemical effects, introducing room for experimentaton to find more powerful brews with the effects we seek. But if you've ever wanted to skip past all that tinkering, this app lets you do so in your web browser.

Search for ingredients by name by typing into the text field below and hitting Enter. This will filter the list of ingredients to show only those containing the provided text in their name. Leaving the field blank will simply show all the ingredients, barring the use of other filters.

If you're looking for any ingredient with a particular combination of effects, use the four dropdown menus below the name search. The results will be updated to show only ingredients that have each of the selected effects.

Finally, this app includes the patched ingredients from Beyond Skyrim: Bruma and Hunterborn. To include these in your search results, click the associated checkboxes for each.
